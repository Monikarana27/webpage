2. The Summer Internship Program:

The summer internship program is offered at the University of Oxford. The program is open to all the current and matriculated oxford students irrespective of their nationality. The internships can be remote as well as in person depending on the preference of the student. These are actually a combination of internships provided by NGOs or research institutions to the students of Oxford.

3. The Visiting Student Research Program:

The VSRP internship program is provided by the King Abdullah University of Science and Technology(KAUST). KAUST is a renowned university in Saudi Arabia and through this internship, it aims to provide opportunities to international students. Students from close to 130 countries participate in this internship program. The university requires at least a 3.5 CGPA to apply for the internship. 200 plus research projects are offered to students to explore.
